About

Use this map to explore all 469 miles of the Blue Ridge Parkway as it winds its way from Virginia to North Carolina. Click on icons to find out more information on Parkway facilities including overlooks, lodging, visitors centers and hiking trails. You can also zoom to points of interest using the tools in the upper left corner of the application.

The data for this application was created almost entirely in-house using a variety of raw sources including parkway information and map icons from the National Park Service, hydrography and landcover data from the United States Geological Survey and building footprints and roadways from Open Street Map. Software used to create the application included ArcMap, QGIS, ArcGIS Pro, Adobe Illustrator and Asset Forge for creating 3d building, bridge and tunnel face models.
